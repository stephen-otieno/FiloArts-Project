The Pinnacle of Creative Solutions

Welcome to FiloArts+.

We are a creativity and innovation company that inspires,educates and solves through three powerful pillars: FiloKid, FIloCraft and FiloSolve. Our Mission is to craft a brighter more creative future by empowering young minds, producing world-class art and solving real world challenges.

About

Who we are

FiloArts+ is dedicated to transforming creativity into action. Through FiloKid, we nurture young innovators. In FiloCraft, we craft diverse, sustainable art products. With FiloSolve, we execute impactful projects to solve today's and tomorrow's challenges.

Mission

Our Mission

To nurture young minds into top creativity and innovators. Produce Top-Quality sustainable art and innovation that creates lasting change .

Our Vision

To be the ultimate hub for creativity, innovation and solutions.

Our Values

  • Creativity and Excellence.
  • Innovation and sustainability.
  • Empowerment and Community.


FiloKid

Inspiring the next generation of innovators.


FiloKid complements the school system by turning theory into practice. Through creative and innovation programs, we empower children to think critically, act creatively and make a difference.
